Top CLJ tickets by weighted vote

Date: March 7, 2013

Open CLJ tickets with 1 or more votes, sorted in descending order of their weighted vote.  Suppose someone has currently voted on N open tickets.  Then their vote counts as 1/N for each of those tickets.  Thus voting on all tickets has the same relative effect on their ranking as voting on no tickets.  You must be selective to change the rankings.
